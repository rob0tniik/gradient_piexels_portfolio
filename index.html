Intro

Welcome to my little one-man post-production facility!

As a dedicated video editor and colorist, I have honed my skills to create visually stunning and emotionally captivating content for couples on their special day. My mission is to capture the essence of their love story and present it in a way that will leave a lasting impression on them and their guests.

Color grading plays a crucial role in the post-production process, as it sets the tone and mood for the video, evoking the emotions you want your viewers to experience. My expertise in color grading allows me to enhance the color of your wedding footage, creating a unique and memorable atmosphere that reflects the joy and excitement of your special day.

In addition to providing exceptional color grading services for weddings, I also cater to other professionals in the audiovisual industry. Over the years, I have successfully collaborated with corporate companies, filmmakers, advertisement agencies, and production studios worldwide. My expertise in color grading and post-production enables me to deliver high-quality and consistent results, regardless of the project's scope or complexity.

Whether you are a fellow videographer, a production company, or an agency, my services can help elevate your projects, ensuring that the final output not only meets but exceeds your expectations. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out to discuss how my color grading and post-production services can enhance your next audiovisual project. Together, we can create visually stunning content that leaves a lasting impression on your target audience.

About

Hello! My name is Alex Lezaun, a passionate video editor and colorist based in Spain. With over five years of experience in the audiovisual industry, I have developed a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of color grading techniques. Throughout my career, I have worked on numerous projects, ranging from weddings to corporate videos and advertisements. My goal is to create visually stunning and emotionally captivating content that resonates with viewers and leaves a lasting impression.

As a dedicated professional, I constantly seek to improve my skills and stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the industry. My commitment to excellence and my passion for storytelling have enabled me to work with a diverse range of clients, delivering high-quality results that exceed their expectations.

In addition to my professional accomplishments, I take pride in being a reliable and easy-to-work-with collaborator. I believe that effective communication and teamwork are crucial to achieving success in the audiovisual industry, and I strive to build strong relationships with my clients and partners.
